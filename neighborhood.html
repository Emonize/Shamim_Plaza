The Neighborhood

Discover the vibrant, highly connected surroundings of South Shahi Eidgah.

0.1 km Away

Shahi Eidgah

The Shahi Eidgah in Sylhet, Bangladesh, is a historic open prayer hall built during the Mughal era. It serves as a significant religious and cultural landmark, hosting thousands of worshippers during Eid celebrations. The Eidgah is known for its architectural beauty and is located approximately three kilometers northeast of the Guru Nanak International Circuit House.

2.0 km Away

Hazrat Shahjalal Mazar

The spiritual heart of Sylhet. The magnificent shrine of Hazrat Shahjalal is one of the most revered and historically significant sites in the region. Located just a short drive from Shamim Plaza, it offers a serene, culturally rich atmosphere deeply embedded in the identity of the city.

3.5 km Away

Keane Bridge & Clock Tower

Spanning the beautiful Surma River, the iconic Keane Bridge and the historic Ali Amjad Clock Tower represent the architectural heritage of Sylhet. This bustling area is a testament to the city's growth, offering breathtaking sunset views over the water.

3.5 km Away

Int'l Cricket Stadium

Renowned as one of the most picturesque cricket grounds in the world. Surrounded by rolling green hills and lush tea gardens, the stadium brings international excitement and a premium sporting atmosphere right to our doorstep.

7.2 km Away

Osmani Airport

Seamless global connectivity. Osmani International Airport ensures that whether you are a resident traveling abroad or an international business client visiting Shamim Plaza, your journey is quick, convenient, and completely stress-free.
